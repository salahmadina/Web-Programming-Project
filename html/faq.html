Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SparkleWash

How do I make a booking?

Browse our car wash locations, click "Book Now" on the one you like, choose a date (today or tomorrow) and a time slot, then confirm.

Can I book for any date?

You can only book for today or tomorrow. Same-day bookings are subject to availability.

What happens if I am late?

If you do not arrive within 10 minutes of your scheduled time, your booking will be automatically cancelled and the slot released.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes. Go to "My Bookings" and click the Cancel button next to any pending booking.

What is the difference between Basic, Premium, and Full Service?

Basic covers exterior wash only. Premium adds window cleaning and wheel scrub. Full Service includes interior vacuuming, waxing, and an air freshener. Check our Gallery page for the full breakdown.

Is my payment information secure?

SparkleWash does not currently process payments online. Payment is made on-site at the car wash location.
