About SparkWash

Online Car Wash Booking System

Project Description

SparkWash is a full-stack web application that lets customers book car wash appointments online. Customers can browse wash packages, choose a time slot, and track their booking status in real time. Staff and administrators manage daily bookings, assign workers, and monitor payments through a simple dashboard.

User Roles

👤 Customer 🧹 Wash Staff 🛠 Administrator

Main Features

  • Online Booking
  • Package Selection
  • Booking Tracking
  • Payment Records
  • Staff Management
  • Customer Reviews
  • Admin Dashboard
  • Booking History

Our Goal

To make car washing as easy as possible — no queuing, no phone calls. Just open the app, book a slot, and arrive to a spotless car.
